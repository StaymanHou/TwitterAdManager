TaskHelper¶

This is the helper for Lib.Task.Task. Dealing with the operations related to Tasks.

Lib.TaskHelper.update_taskqueue(task_queue, twitter_sessions)¶

Update the task_queue. In following order:

Lib.TaskFactory.TaskFactory.get_delete_tasks()

Lib.TaskFactory.TaskFactory.get_create_tasks()

Then, if it is another hour, Lib.TaskFactory.TaskFactory.get_local_update_task()
